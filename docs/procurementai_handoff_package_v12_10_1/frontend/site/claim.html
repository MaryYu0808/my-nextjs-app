Before you request updates

  • Scope: AI-enabled procurement companies (procurement-native or procurement-first). General-purpose automation/BI platforms are typically excluded.
  • Authorized reps only: Please submit using a work email address from your company domain.
  • Evidence required: Link to official pages, docs, releases, or public sources that support your requested changes.
  • Neutral directory: Claiming does not imply ranking, endorsement, or partnership.
  • If your company is not listed yet, use Submit a Company.

Verification & review

  • We check that your work email domain matches the company website domain.
  • We send an email confirmation link to verify authorization.
  • Our team reviews sources and requested changes before updating the profile.
  • Once approved, we update the profile and refresh Data last verified.
  • Typical turnaround: 3–5 business days after confirmation.

What we update

  • Factual profile info (website, HQ, founded year)
  • Workflow category, use cases, and automation maturity (taxonomy mapping)
  • Key modules and official source links
